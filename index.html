Welcome to the portfolio of Maeve

Hello, I'm Meave and this is some information about my work.

My emphasis is on intagration of digital methods while keeping the hand present in the finished work.

I graduated from Moore College of Art in Philadelphia in 2013 with a major in Fine Art. My work has always integrated digital methods with a tactile sensibility.

My current work is focused on the history and visual language of textile disign. I strive to continue the history and tradition of this industry while also moving forward to the 21th century.
